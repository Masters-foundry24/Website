Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the standard of the painting?

We don't have different levels of painting. We paint everything to the same high standard. You can see plenty of examples of this in the gallery.

Where are you based?

São Tomé and Princípe is an independent island nation in the Gulf of Guinea. With a GDP per capita of $3,167 São Tomé is still developing but we think it has a lot of potencial. Our founder Scott Masters is originally from Australia. He chose São Tomé as the ideal spot to locate the company.

Who are your customers?

We work with businesses who what to make painted miniatures available to their clients. We let you handle sales and marketing so that we can focus on our real obsession painting to the highest standard possible.

Are you currently taking commissions?

Yes! If you are interested in working with us please contact us to start a conversation.

What are your shipping times?

After you pass along a client's order your miniatures will be shipped to you within a week. Then there is additional postage time:
- Europe & UK: 10 - 15 working days
- North America: 15- 20 working days
- Australia & New Zealand: 15 - 25 working days
We use international resisted mail which includes track and trace in most countries.

What import taxes apply to São Tomé?

As a democratic developing country São Tomé qualifies for programs such as AGOA (US) and EBA (EU) granting duty free access. Countries may still charge VAT at the boarder but there are generally no tariffs on top of this. For full details see our logistics page.

What are your prices?

Our prices are able to be very competitive due to our own efficiency the low cost of your own inputs. The prices we quote vary with the amount of business you can direct our way so please contact us if you would like to know more.
