Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

What is the standard of the painting?

We don't have different levels of painting. We paint everything to the same high standard. You can see plenty of examples of this in the gallery.

What if I am not satisfied with my miniatures?

We will send photos of your miniature before we ship and we are happy to make changes if you are not satisfied. Once we ship is it usually not possible to make them again, unless there is damage during transport.

I have a special requirement for my miniature.

We love a challenge! Reach out to us via our contact and tell us your grand idea. We are yet to meet a vision we couldn't create.

Where are you based?

The printing and painting takes place in tropical São Tomé.

How long will my miniatures take to arrive?

After you order your miniatures will be shipped to you within a week. Then there is additional postage time:
- Europe & UK: 7 - 12 working days
- North America: 10 - 20 working days
- Australia & New Zealand: 15 - 25 working days

Do packages have track and trace?

We use international registered mail. As far as we know we can track your package in every country except Australia. For Australian packages you will still know when the package leaves from Portugal to Australia.
