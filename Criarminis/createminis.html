Create Minis


In creating your own miniatures you have lots of tools to choose from. Here are a few of our favourites. To show the flexibility of each platform we have designed everyone’s favourite grey pilgrim in each one.

Hero Forge

Hero Forge has a really simple and intuitive UI that also manages to offer great flexibility to you, the designer. Buying the .stl of your model usually costs $8 US but you can also get the config of your model for free, send it us and we will print it for just $3 extra out. Heroforge has a really simple and intuitive UI that also manages to offer great flexibility to you, the designer. Buying the .stl of your model usually costs $8 US but you can also get the config of your model for free, send it us and we will print it for just $3 extra (our cost to buy the .stl with a subscription). Heroforge also has a feature to colour your minis allowing a simple way to tell us exactly how you want them painted.

Eldritch Foundry

Eldritch Foundry is a bit cheaper than Heroforge. You can buy a single miniature for $5 US or a months subscription with unlimited downloads for $15 US. On the downside they usually offer fewer options for the designer to mix and match. We do adore the ability to ride a Dune Maw.

Titan Craft

Titan craft has improved a lot in the past few years with a much faster and more beautiful UI. They have a unique payment model where you purchase packs of assets (different types of hats, swords, claws and whatever else) and then you can use these assets on as many free downloads as you like. There is a decent selection of free assets so beginners may be able to create some excellent miniatures without paying a cent.

ANVL

ANVL is the last miniature designer on our list. They have an impressive list of assets to mix and match but they fall behind Hero Forge in the ability to fine tune your mini and in our experience their site can run a little slow. Each miniature costs $5 US.

My Mini Factory

Finally, if you’re looking for some pre-designed miniatures to get started at once then My Mini Factory has a great selection including some free downloads.
